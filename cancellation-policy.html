Cancellation Policy

We understand things come up and can often cause delays or event cancellations in scheduled events. For this reason we have implemented a cancellation policy that's made to be fair on both the customer side as well as our company side. All customers have 5 business days to cancel their reservation from the date and time of the event. This will allow your deposit to be refunded in full. If cancelled after that, then we'll offer you an option to reschedule. Once our equipment has been installed on site, than no cancellation will be allowed, and the event will be billed at the set rate. We reserve the right to cancel any event for any reason that we find necessary. If we happen to see severe weather such as high winds, lightening, or rain in our area than we normally will be forced to cancel the rental for everyones safety, unless of course its indoors.
